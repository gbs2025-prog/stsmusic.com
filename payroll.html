    What is a Payroll Management System?

    A Payroll Management System (PMS) helps businesses manage employee salaries, benefits, taxes, and deductions in an automated and efficient manner.

    Key Features

    • Salary Calculation
    • Tax and Deductions Management
    • Employee Benefits Management
    • Direct Deposit Integration

    Benefits

    PMS reduces errors, saves time, and ensures compliance with tax regulations.
